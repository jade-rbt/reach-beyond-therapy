Who We Are

About Reach Beyond Therapy

A boutique hand therapy practice built around you — not a system.

Our Philosophy

Hands Are More Than Just Tools

"Hands are more than just tools — they're how you work, connect, and live."

At Reach Beyond Therapy, we believe that healing is personal. Every patient brings a unique story, a unique body, and a unique set of goals. That's why we take the time to truly understand who you are and what recovery means to you — before we ever start treatment.

Our approach is rooted in trust. We build long-term relationships with our clients, providing ongoing support beyond the walls of the clinic. Recovery doesn't end when your session does, and neither does our commitment to your progress.

[About RBT Image]
Our Mission

Helping You Move Beyond Limitations — Back to What You Love

We are here to walk alongside you through every stage of recovery — rebuilding strength, restoring mobility, and reclaiming the resilience that makes you who you are.

Whether you're healing from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or recovering from an acute injury, Reach Beyond Therapy brings structure where it counts and adaptability where it matters most.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Hand & Upper Extremity Care

From in-office sessions to in-home visits, we meet you where you are.

🏥

In-Office Therapy

Located in Redondo Beach, our clinic offers a private, focused environment for your treatment sessions with full access to specialized equipment and modalities.

🏠

In-Home Visits

For patients with transportation challenges or who prefer the comfort and privacy of their own home, we offer in-home hand therapy services throughout the South Bay.

🛠️

Custom Splinting

We design and fabricate custom orthoses on-site, molded directly to your hand for the most precise fit and optimal therapeutic outcomes.

🔬

Post-Operative Rehab

Coordinated closely with your surgeon, our post-operative protocols are designed to protect your repair and progressively restore full function.

Why Choose Us

A People-First Practice

Unlike large therapy chains where you're passed between aides and assistants, at Reach Beyond Therapy you work directly with your Certified Hand Therapist for the full duration of every session.

We take the time to listen, to learn your story, and to build a treatment plan that truly fits your life — not just your diagnosis. Our clients become more than patients; they become part of our community.

  • ✓ 1-on-1 sessions — you always see your therapist
