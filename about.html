About Reach Beyond Therapy

A boutique hand therapy practice built around you.

Our Philosophy

Hands Are More Than Just Tools

"Hands are more than just tools — they're how you work, connect, and live."

Reach Beyond Therapy is a boutique hand therapy practice dedicated to delivering expert, personalized care in a setting that puts you first. Our clinic-based approach gives every patient direct, uninterrupted time with their therapist — because that's how real recovery happens.

Our hands are more than just functional tools — they're our connection to independence, creativity, work, and loved ones. Whether you're striving to return to your job, your hobbies, or simply pain-free daily life, our mission is to help you rebuild strength, restore mobility, and reclaim your resilience.

We believe real healing happens when therapy goes beyond the surface — when it's personal, purposeful, and rooted in trust.

Our Mission

Helping You Move Beyond Limitations — Back to What You Love

We are here to walk alongside you through every stage of recovery — rebuilding strength, restoring mobility, and reclaiming the resilience that makes you who you are.

Whether you're healing from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or recovering from an acute injury, Reach Beyond Therapy brings structure where it counts and adaptability where it matters most.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Hand & Upper Extremity Care

Evidence-based care delivered in a focused, one-on-one clinical setting.

🏥

In-Office Therapy

Located in Redondo Beach, our clinic offers a private, focused environment for your treatment sessions with full access to specialized equipment and modalities.

🏠

In-Home Visits

In-home visits are available upon request for patients with specific needs or transportation challenges. Please reach out to discuss whether this option is right for your situation.

🛠️

Custom Splinting

We design and fabricate custom orthoses on-site, molded directly to your hand for the most precise fit and optimal therapeutic outcomes.

🔬

Post-Operative Rehab

Coordinated closely with your surgeon, our post-operative protocols are designed to protect your repair and progressively restore full function.
