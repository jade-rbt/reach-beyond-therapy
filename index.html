What We Treat

Specialized Hand Therapy

Hand therapy is a specialized form of rehabilitation focused on the hand, wrist, elbow, and shoulder. Our Certified Hand Therapist has the advanced training to treat a wide range of conditions — from acute injuries to chronic conditions — using evidence-based techniques tailored to your recovery goals.

We treat fractures, tendon injuries, carpal tunnel syndrome, arthritis, nerve conditions, post-surgical rehabilitation, and much more.
