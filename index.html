What We Treat

Specialized Hand Therapy

Hand therapy is a concentrated area within occupational and physical therapy that addresses injuries and conditions of the hand and upper extremities.

It aims to improve or restore hand and arm function, enabling individuals to perform daily tasks, work-related activities, and recreational pursuits.

Hand therapy helps individuals recover from fractures, dislocations, tendon and nerve injuries, burns, and crush injuries. We also manage conditions like arthritis, carpal tunnel syndrome, Dupuytren's contracture, and other conditions that affect hand and upper limb function. Lastly, hand therapy plays a crucial role in rehabilitation after hand or upper limb surgeries.
