Hi there! My name is Jade, I am an occupational therapist and certified hand therapist. I am so grateful to have been featured by VoyageLA as one of South Bay's Hidden Gems, to tell my story and describe a little bit of my business.

The journey came with factors of my personal life and the big picture: who do I want to be and where am I going? Ultimately, I stepped away from high volume clinics where patients have to end up coming to excessive and unnecessary visits so that I can step into my true calling of honing my passion for hand therapy and applying it to high quality one-on-one care.

I provide patients with truly personalized sessions and paths to full recovery with their greatest tools to connect with life: **their hands**.

We help people rebuild and restore their upper extremity strength and function. Book a free consult call if you're wondering what hand therapy can do for you.
