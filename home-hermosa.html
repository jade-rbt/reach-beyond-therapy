Built for Hermosa's Active Community

We Treat the Injuries That Come with the Lifestyle

Hermosa Beach runs on movement — surfing, beach volleyball, pickleball, running the strand, cycling. The repetitive demands of that lifestyle create specific upper extremity injuries that general physical therapy often doesn't fully understand.

We treat TFCC tears and wrist sprains from surfing, scaphoid fractures, finger dislocations and ligament sprains from volleyball, and overuse conditions common to cyclists and paddlers. We also see post-surgical patients and everyday overuse cases from non-athletic life.
