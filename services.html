Our Core Services

How We Help You Recover

Certified Hand Therapy

Our foundational service covers non-operative and post-operative care for conditions of the hand, wrist, elbow, and shoulder. Every treatment plan is individualized and evidence-based.

Treatment techniques include:

  • Wound management & scar tissue treatment
  • Swelling reduction (edema management)
  • Daily activity retraining (ADL training)
  • Therapeutic exercise programs
  • Pain control strategies
  • Soft tissue mobilization
  • Taping techniques
  • Joint mobilization
  • Sensation & nerve injury training
  • Lymphedema management
  • Custom splint fabrications

Special Services

We offer a range of advanced and specialized therapeutic modalities to complement your treatment plan and accelerate your recovery.

Specialized modalities include:

  • Thermal modalities (heat & cold therapy)
  • Electrical stimulation (NMES, TENS)
  • Graston technique (instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization)
  • Cupping therapy
  • Tuning fork therapy
  • Laser treatments (low-level laser therapy)

These treatments are selected based on your specific condition and therapeutic goals, and are always delivered by a Certified Hand Therapist.

Custom Splinting Fabrication

We design and fabricate custom orthoses on-site for fingers, hands, wrists, and elbows using thermoplastic materials molded directly to your anatomy.

Splints for conditions including:

  • Dislocations & fractures
  • Post-surgical immobilization
  • Nerve injuries
  • Joint conditions & arthritis
  • Tendon repairs
  • Chronic conditions
