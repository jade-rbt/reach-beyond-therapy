Insurance Reimbursement

We Make It as Straightforward as Possible

While we do not participate as in-network providers, we do courtesy insurance verification checks to see if — and what — your out-of-network benefits will cover. You have two options:

Option 1

Self-Submit Reimbursement

At the end of your session, we provide a detailed receipt including insurance billing codes (a superbill) that you may submit to your PPO insurance for potential reimbursement. Please note — a prescription from your doctor may be required depending on your policy.

Option 2

Courtesy Billing

Prefer a hands-off approach? You can elect for courtesy billing, where we submit your insurance claims on your behalf — so you can focus on your recovery instead of paperwork.

Flexible Payment Options

We accept all major credit cards (Amex, Discover, Visa), Venmo, Zelle, and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) or Health Savings Accounts (HSA).
